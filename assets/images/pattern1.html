12 Free High Resolution Grunge Stripes Photoshop Patterns

Posted on 16. May, 2009
by WebTreats in Patterns, Textures

Today we have another conveniently packaged Photoshop pattern set (.pat). This time they are made from slightly tweaked versions of 12 of our favorite patterns in the Tileable Grunge Stripes Background Set on Backgrounds ETC.

They range in resolution from 512px*512px to 1024px*1024px

Download the 12 Free High Resolution Fabric Photoshop Patterns Below.


If you’re looking for a particular color that isn’t in this set let us know or head over to Backgrounds ETC where you will find the full set of over 160 tileable grunge stripes backgrounds in jpg format.
